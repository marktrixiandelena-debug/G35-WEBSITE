# User Persona

The user persona for the **Barangay Calauag Flood and Drainage Reporting System** is a proactive and community-conscious resident living in a flood-prone area of Naga City. They are often concerned about the impact of the rainy season on their home and neighborhood. For them, accessibility, transparency, and accountability are paramount.

They lead a life that is frequently disrupted by unpredictable flooding events. They value a user-friendly interface that allows them to quickly document and report incidents even under the stress of an ongoing storm. Real-time status tracking and a formal record of their reports are essential features for this persona, as they seek assurance that their voice is being heard by the local government. 

They have encountered frustrations with informal, unrecorded reporting methods in the past—where reports are often overlooked or lost in unmonitored social media threads—and are looking for a dependable, centralized solution to ensure their community's safety and infrastructure needs are met.

This user persona serves as a foundational representation of the target user for the system, helping to guide design and development decisions to meet their specific preferences and life-saving requirements.
